Privacy Policy for SnippetBase

Last updated: March 16, 2026

SnippetBase is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our handling of your information within the application.

1. Data Collection

SnippetBase does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not have access to the information you save or process within the app.

2. Local Storage

All text snippets you create are stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. This data is not synced to any cloud service, external server, or third-party database by us.

3. Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with third parties. The app does not include any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks that collect user behavior data.

4. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored exclusively on your device, you have full control over it. Deleting the SnippetBase application will permanently remove all your saved snippets and associated data from your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
